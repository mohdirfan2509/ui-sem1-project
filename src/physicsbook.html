  • Ashish Arora sir

    Ashish Arora is a highly respected physics educator and author, known for his extensive contributions to competitive exam preparation. He is the founder of Physics Galaxy, an online platform providing high-quality physics resources for students preparing for IIT-JEE, NEET, and international physics Olympiads. With over three decades of teaching experience, he has mentored numerous top-ranking students. He has authored the Physics Galaxy book series, which covers a vast range of physics topics in a structured and comprehensive manner. His YouTube channel, Physics Galaxy, has helped millions of students worldwide by offering free video lectures, making physics more accessible and engaging

  • 700+ Advanced Illustrations in Physics for JEE Advanced is a comprehensive guide designed to help students master complex physics concepts through visual learning. The book features more than 700 high-quality illustrations and problem-solving techniques tailored for JEE Advanced aspirants. It covers mechanics, electromagnetism, optics, waves, and modern physics with a step-by-step approach. Each topic is supplemented with in-depth explanations and shortcut methods to enhance conceptual clarity and problem-solving speed. This book serves as an essential resource for students aiming to excel in JEE Advanced physics.
